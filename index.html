What factors are sales related to?

This web page draws on relevant data from the github project "Weather-and-Sales-Prediction-Analysis".

The data source is the sales data of an Irish Midlands fast food restaurant in Ireland from May 2021 to May 2024. Through visualization, you can view the changes in sales of each product and its relationship with the week and day, correlation of underlying factors such as weather.

This is a stacked chart. The abscissa is date and the ordinate is sales. Based on the stacked sales of various products, the chart automatically plays back according to time.

You can customize the start date of the chart through state date, select the x-axis to display the date span through Period, and the refresh button will redraw based on the first two.

The stop and start buttons pause and start playback respectively. When the mouse hovers over the x-axis date value, the right prompt bar displays the sales data of the corresponding date.

Does weather have anything to do with sales? For me the weather often affects my mood and appetite, but does it apply to others?


To this end, a dashboard of sales and weather conditions is drawn. The angle represents the date. The sales data is drawn as a line chart on the inside of the disk. The first layer of the outer circle represents the average temperature of the day, and the color from blue to red represents the low to high temperature; The second layer is the sunshine time, and the third layer is the precipitation amount. The degree of the two is represented by the size of the scatter points.

From the disk chart, we can only roughly see the daily weather and sales. From the previous data, we can observe that sales have a certain correlation with the week.

In order to eliminate this relationship and observe more intuitively, I calculated each The average sales volume of each food in the month is displayed in the form of a line chart.

At the same time, the average weather conditions, including average temperature, sunshine duration and precipitation, are calculated and displayed in the form of a histogram; the above two types of information can be displayed through the options Frame your own selection for observation.
